Open Science Newsletter 08/2023

Dear colleagues,
this is the N newsletter by the Open Science Office at the CIMH. We offer resources and guidance with respect to open science questions. You can find further information in the attached flyer and on the intranet: https://intranet.zi-mannheim.de/forschung/ref-fors-strategie-fors-foerd/open-science-office
Call for news, updates and Open Science relevant opportunities
We aim to keep you updated with potentially relevant opportunities regarding Open Science activities at the ZIhub. If you think of anything Open Science-relevant to share, send it along to us and we will include it in the next newsletter. For example, if you have ideas for speakers in the Open Science Colloquium. Please email us directly with topics you think would be beneficial to the group -- happy to help spread the word.


Best regards,


Your Open Science Office team
Dr. Gordon Feld, Dr. Melanie Fritz and Dr. Anita Schick
